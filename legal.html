Corporate Event Framework

Verification RC 8997727

15D Sky Party™ acts as the Corporate Event Host and Charterer. We do not provide air transport services directly; rather, we secure whole-aircraft charter capacity from NCAA-licensed Air Operator’s Certificate (AOC) holders to facilitate a private networking environment for our vetted members.

  • / Charterer Identity: Sky Party LTD (RC 8997727) as the primary event host.
  • / Aviation Partner: All flights executed by licensed AOC holders under Nig. CARs Part 9.
  • / Passenger Rights: Fully protected under Nig. CARs Part 1.3 protections.
  • / Compliance: Strict adherence to ICAO safety briefings and NAFDAC catering standards.
  • / Logistics Split: Automated disbursement ensuring aviation fees reach the operator directly.
  • / NDA Protocols: Binding confidentiality required for all 15th Dimension manifest attendees.
